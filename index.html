Yolow

Notification System for Laptop Battery Life

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

Ever find yourself in a situation where you leave you laptop without the charger plugged in and you go watch television or something similar only to find that it's DEAD when you come back? What if you could save it's life? Now you can receive an INSTANT notification when your battery is low and needs to be plugged in even when you're not near it! Introducing YOLOw!
